		&  \ce{Br2_{(g)}} \qquad  & ${}\leftrightharpoons{}$ \qquad & 2\ce{Br_{(g)}}  \\
		\midrule
		Início   &       0,0456 mol/2,3 L (0,0198 M)    &&   $\cdots$                           \\
